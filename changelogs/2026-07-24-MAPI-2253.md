## What Changed

- OpenAPI entry is upgraded to 3.2.0 with contact and tag navigation metadata; product-policy prose (versioning, rate limits, idempotency) is left out pending product confirmation.
- Spec hygiene is enforceable in CI: invalid examples, missing security/descriptions/4xx, and controlled-vocabulary terms fail `npm run lint`; trailing-slash URL rules stay warn-only pending a contract decision.
- Published docs offer YAML and JSON OpenAPI downloads from Pages (`./openapi.yaml`, `./openapi.json`) via ReDoc `downloadUrls`; blank/wrong-language code-sample stubs are removed and remaining gaps are tracked in `spec/code_samples/TODO.md`.

## Why

Warn-only lint and noisy/incorrect OpenAPI artifacts let documentation regressions merge and block reliable client and agent integrations, without inventing unverified product policy in public docs.
